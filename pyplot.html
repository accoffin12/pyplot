Answer Overiview:¶

There are actually two methods that can be used to answer this question. One that utilizes pandas and the other a quick overview. There are advantages and disadvantages to each.

Pandas DataFrames:¶

With the first method utilizing pandas, the text is transfered into a DataFrame that can be referenced later and utilized for a larger amount of analysis. While in this example it isn't as critical, when dealing with machine learning or heavy statistical analysis transfering data into a dataframe allows for a larger amount of manipulation. Quick Method:¶

The quick method that's demonstrated in the second box allows us to obtain an over view of the data, and have a little more control in terms of how the plot is created. On the bright side this method allows for us to quickly obtain a view of a graph.
